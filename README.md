### ◇ &nbsp;research

I build and align **multimodal foundation models** — teaching
systems to perceive across vision, language, and audio, then
sharpening their behavior through post-training and reinforcement
learning, and serving them fast.

- **VLM** — visual perception & grounded reasoning
- **Post-training** — SFT, preference alignment, distillation
- **RL** — RLHF / RLVR, reward modeling, agentic policies
- **KV cache** — efficient long-context inference & serving
- **TTS** — expressive, low-latency speech synthesis
- **3D** — spatial reasoning & generation
